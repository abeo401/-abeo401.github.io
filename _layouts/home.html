Abeosystem Co., Ltd is a laboratory venture company that was founded on March 1, 2014. The company was founded by Professor Lee Byung-ryong (Intelligent Mechatronics Lab, Department of Mechanical Engineering at the University of Ulsan, Ulsan, South Korea) and Dr. Do Van Phu. Although the company it is a small company with 2 employees, but it is trying to develop and apply software that can boast world-class performance (precision, calculation speed) in the field of 3D scanner. 3D scanner is a state-of-the-art measuring equipment that scans a specific part of an object in three dimensions to calculate the three-dimensional data in a short time, such as the dimension or shape of the object. Using 3D scanner, welding defect inspection, shape defect inspection, 3D shape restoration, design, and design can be performed, so there are many applications.
